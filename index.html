What We Do

You used to be able to list yourself in the Yellow Pages and the work would just flow in. But times have changed. In the 21st century you need a much more comprehensive marketing strategy. One that needs to incorporate modern technology trends.

Our services fall into several broad categories:

Planning

New projects offer the most flexibility and lowest cost. We are able to help you start things off on the right foot with a professional project plan. We work with you to analyze business needs for a new project and create a roadmap complete with branding and code elements.

You choose whether to take this plan and implement it yourself, or utilize our custom design and development services.

Although new projects often benefit the most from this roadmapping process, we can also help when projects stray off-track.

Design & Development

We break down seeming complex project requirements into a prioritized action plan. You have full control of priority (though some tasks are related and will have prerequisites that need to be completed first). Often we identify a particular project to be comprised of multiple sub-projects: some static and some dynamic. Reimplementing things as a series of the smallest possible subsets of features will often speed up planning, design and development, minimize costs, and maximize return on investment.

As an example, we have separated out our website functionality into a static marketing website (this site which isn't frequently updated), a blog (regularly updated content, but infrequently updated layout), and a series of small web applications (fully dynamic, often able to be productized). This provides a maximum amount of flexibility during and after development.
